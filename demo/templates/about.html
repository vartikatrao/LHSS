Homomorphic Secret Sharing (HSS) is a cutting-edge cryptographic technique designed to enhance data privacy and security in cloud environments. This project explores the use of Lattice-based Homomorphic Secret Sharing (LHSS) to enable secure computations on encrypted data without exposing the underlying information. By splitting data into multiple encrypted shares and distributing them across different parties, our system ensures that no single entity can access the complete data. Computations are performed collaboratively and securely, with only the final result being revealed. This approach is particularly valuable in privacy-sensitive applications such as healthcare, finance, and secure data analytics, offering a robust solution against data breaches and unauthorized access in the cloud.
